Work in MVIG: Hand Mesh reconstruction based on RGB Image (will be submitted to BMVC)

An NN model is applied to the image to get the pose and the shape of the hand. My partner and I use the hourglass network to generate the 3d heatmap of the joints of the hand and the depth map of the hand. The intermediate results are the 2d heatmap of the joints and the mask of the hand. We calculate the loss between the estimated value and the groundtruth value of the joints and the depth-map and the mask. Using the loss, we train the neural network. From the estimated depth-map and the joints, we do regression to fit a parametric hand mesh called MANO to meet the estimation.
